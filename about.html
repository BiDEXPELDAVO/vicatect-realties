ABOUT VICAGTECT REALTIES

Vicagtect is a renowned construction company that was established in 2007. With over 16 years of experience in the industry, we have earned a solid reputation for delivering high-quality construction projects across various sectors.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction have been the driving forces behind our success. At Vicagtect, we specialize in providing comprehensive construction services, including design-build, general contracting, construction management, and project development. We have successfully completed a wide range of projects, from residential and commercial buildings to infrastructure and industrial facilities.
